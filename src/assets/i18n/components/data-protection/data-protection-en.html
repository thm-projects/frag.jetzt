1. Principle

Your personal data is treated confidentially. All actions comply with legal regulations, particularly the EU General Data Protection Regulation (EU-GDPR), the Hessian Data Protection Act (HDSG), and the Telemedia Act (TMG). The operator of frag.jetzt ensures compliance with these standards.

2. Data Collection and Use

2.1 Usage Data

Personal data related to the use of frag.jetzt is collected, processed, and used only as necessary to provide the platform. Registration is generally not required, with the following exceptions:

  • If you want to store your own rooms permanently.
  • If you are to be permanently added as a moderator to a room.

In these cases, a valid email address is stored as a login name. Room creators who add moderators have access to their email addresses.

Guest rooms and bonuses are deleted as soon as the browser's web storage is cleared. Rooms created by registered users are linked to their accounts and can be deleted by them. Inactive rooms are automatically deleted 180 days after the last visit.

2.2 Access Data

Only technically necessary personal data is collected. Apart from the email address used as a login name, no personal data is stored.

3. AI-Based Moderation

frag.jetzt offers an optional AI-based moderation feature for forum posts. Room creators and moderators can enable or disable this function in the settings. When enabled, local moderation models and the OpenAI Moderation API are used to analyze content.

The moderation checks posts for toxic language, hate speech, or other problematic content. Additionally, AI can categorize posts based on their sentiment as positive, neutral, or negative. The goal of this feature is to promote a respectful and constructive discussion culture on frag.jetzt.

4. Hosting and Data Security

4.1 External Hosting

frag.jetzt is hosted by Hetzner Online GmbH in Germany. All data collected on the platform is stored on its servers. Hetzner undergoes annual audits by TÃœV Rheinland to ensure compliance with security standards under Article 32 of the GDPR.

4.2 Log Files

Information automatically transmitted by your browser is stored. This includes:

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Referrer URL
  • IP address
  • Hostname of the accessing computer
  • Time of server request

These data are not linked to specific individuals and are not combined with other data sources. Log data is stored for a maximum of 30 days.

5. Web Analytics with Matomo

frag.jetzt uses Matomo, an open-source software for anonymous analysis of user behavior. Matomo uses cookies stored locally on your device, allowing analysis of frag.jetzt usage. The IP address is anonymized immediately after processing. No data is shared with third parties.

6. Storage in Web Storage

frag.jetzt uses web storage techniques to store user-specific settings and interactions. The following information may be stored locally in your browser:

  • Acceptance of the privacy policy
  • Language and display settings
  • Onboarding status
  • Login information (guest/registered user)
  • Visited rooms and assigned roles
  • Activation of post functions

These data can be viewed and deleted locally in your browser.

7. Use of AI Assistance

frag.jetzt offers various AI models for use. Your inputs may be forwarded to external AI models for processing:

  • DeepSeek-R1 (Distilled 14B): Operated locally on our servers, does not store usage data, and has no internet access.
  • OpenAI Models (e.g., GPT-4o and o3-mini): Your queries are sent to OpenAI servers and are subject to their privacy policies. OpenAI may store data for error analysis.
  • Use of Personal API Keys: If you use your own API key, processing is subject to the privacy policies of your chosen provider.

8. Data Sharing with Third Parties

No data is shared with third parties unless legally required. If external service providers have access to data, contractual, technical, and organizational measures ensure compliance with data protection laws.

9. External Content and Links

Users can embed external content (e.g., YouTube or Vimeo videos) at various points. Loading such content may transmit data to the respective provider. Please refer to the privacy policies of these providers:

  • Google/Youtube
  • Vimeo

10. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request free access to your stored personal data,
  • Correct, block, or delete your data,
  • Revoke your consent to data processing at any time.

If you have registered on frag.jetzt, you can delete your account at any time. To do this, log in, go to your profile, and click "Delete Account."

11. Changes to the Privacy Policy

This privacy policy may be updated at any time to comply with legal requirements or technical changes.
