Development and Funding

The development of »frag.jetzt« was funded as part of the university network project »Digitally Supported Teaching and Learning in Hesse« by the State of Hesse.

Accessibility Statement

»frag.jetzt« is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for individuals with disabilities. We continuously improve user-friendliness and apply relevant accessibility standards. Particular attention is given to making the platform accessible for visually impaired users.

Compliance Status

The Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) define requirements for designers and developers to improve web accessibility for individuals with disabilities. There are three compliance levels: A, AA, and AAA. »frag.jetzt« is partially compliant with WCAG 2.1 Level AA. Partial compliance means that some parts of the content do not fully conform to the accessibility standard.
