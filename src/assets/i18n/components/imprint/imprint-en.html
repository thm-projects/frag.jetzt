Accessibility Statement

»frag.jetzt« is committed to digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We continually improve usability for everyone and apply relevant accessibility standards. We specifically address visually impaired users of »frag.jetzt«.

Compliance Status

The Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) define requirements for designers and developers to improve access for people with disabilities. There are three levels of compliance: Level A, AA, and AAA. »frag.jetzt« is partially compliant with WCAG 2.1 Level AA. Partial compliance means that some parts of the content do not fully meet the accessibility standard.
